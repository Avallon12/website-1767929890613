Our Philosophy

More Than Just a Meal,
It's a Celebration

At Cantina del Sol, we believe that food is the universal language of love. Founded in 1985 by Chef Maria Gonzalez, our kitchen brings the vibrant streets of Mexico City to your table.

Every tortilla is hand-pressed, every mole is slow-cooked for 24 hours, and every guest is treated like family.
